III. Planning a SparkPoint Center


Overview of Planning Process

Though every SparkPoint centerâ€™s planning time varies, on average it takes about 12 months to fully launch a center after an institution has completed the discovery phase. Multiple factors can influence the planning phase but reaching readiness on the institutional self- assessment with strong buy-in from institution, staff, and students will make the process go smoother.
When launching the planning phase, it is important to start with a strong base.

Planning committee members

This stage is designed to get members engaged, codify relationships, identify a champion, understand SparkPoint model, and define the vision. The outcome of this process is to develop a core group of members who agree to SparkPoint as a concept and commit to participate in the planning process.
