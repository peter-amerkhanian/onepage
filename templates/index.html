The SparkPoint Toolkit

A Guide to replicating SparkPoint

Overview

The SparkPoint Toolkit is intended to help stakeholders regionally and nationally as they plan, pilot and implement SparkPoint to support individuals and families seeking financial prosperity. The Toolkit has been created for you to use, to make your own, and to improve upon as you make your journey from discovery to implementation. As you experience effective ways to apply these tools, please share your learnings with UWBA and its partners across the Bay Area by emailing us at sparkpoint@uwba.org. It is our desire to create a SparkPoint Community of Practice that fosters peer sharing of effective practices and lessons learned with others so that each region is not having to reinvent the wheel.
